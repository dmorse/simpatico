20  * The functions defined in this file are used by function templates that calculate
21  * the isotropic pressure (double), x-y-z diagonal stress components (Vector),
22  * and the full stress tensor (Tensor) using a common template.
23  *
24  * The overloaded incrementPairStress correctly takes care of the addition of the
25  * contribution from the force between a pair of particles to either a double
26  * precision pressure or to a Vector or Tensor of stress components.
27  *
28  * The overloaded normalizeStress function divides the trace of the stress tensor
29  * by 3.0 (the dimensionality of space) for a double precision pressure, but does
30  * nothing when called with a Vector or Tensor of stress components.
31  */
